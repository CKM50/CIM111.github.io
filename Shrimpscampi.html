Shrimp Scampi

Serves 2



As you add each ingrediant, click the bullet to make it go away!


  • 1/2 Pack Linguini Noodles
  • 1 Cup Cooking Wine
  • Pepper
  • Lemon Juice (Squeezed)
  • Butter
  • Complete Seasoning
  • Ole Bay (a dash)
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Garlic Mash

    DIRECTIONS

    As you finish each step, click the bullet to make it go away!

  1. Take a medium sized pot and fill it with water an inch and a half from the brim of the pot. Add in the seasoning to the water. Bring the water to a boil.
  2. Take a little more than half of the linguini from the pasta pack and drop it in the pot once it reaches the boiling point. Let the noodles cook.
  3. In another saucepan, add in olive oil, cooking wine, and butter. Set the stove to medium.
  4. Season the shrimp and add the lemon juice.
  5. Put the shrimp in the saucepan and cook for 4 minutes on medium-high heat.
  6. Place the noodles in the same sauce pan as the shrimp and stir.
  7. Bon Appétit!
