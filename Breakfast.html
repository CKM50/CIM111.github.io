DIRECTIONS

As you finish each step, click the bullet to make it go away!

  1. Drop the olive oil into a saucepan and turn it to medium heat.
  2. Cut the Garcia Sausage into cubes and throw in the pan.
  3. Add the onions into the pan and let the sausage and onions cook for about 4 minutes
  4. Crack 3 eggs into the saucepan and as soon as they start to whiten, scramble them.
  5. Add all the seasoning into the pan and mix.
  6. Bon Appétit!
