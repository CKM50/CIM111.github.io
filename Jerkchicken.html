Slow Cooked Jerk Chicken

Serves 3



As you add each ingrediant, click the bullet to make it go away!


  • Walkerswood Spicy Jerk Marinade (17oz)
  • Jerk Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Crushed Chicken Bullion
  • Tablespoon of Water
  • tablepoon olive oil
  • Curry Powder
  • Red Pepper

DIRECTIONS

As you finish each step, click the bullet to make it go away!

  1. Season the chicken with all the seasonings listed above. Add half of the jerk marinade bottle and refridgerate over night.
  2. Take a slowcooker and put the timer on 8 hours and on low heat.
  3. Add in water, olive oil, and butter into the slowcooker.
  4. Let the wings cook for 8 hours, enjoy your day!
  5. Once your home, use tongs to take out the wings and place them on to a cooking sheet.
  6. Press "Broil" on your oven and let it heat.
  7. Place the cooking sheet in the oven for 5 minutes.
  8. Bon Appétit!
